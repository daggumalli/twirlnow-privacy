Privacy Policy  ·  2026-05-07

What this app does

TwirlNow publishes carousels and videos to the operator's own personal LinkedIn account. It is not a hosted service. It runs on the operator's machine.

Data we access

With your permission, the app reads your LinkedIn profile ID and uses it to publish posts you authored to your own feed. It does not read your connections, messages, or any other account data.

Data we store

The OAuth token LinkedIn issues is stored locally on the operator's machine. It is never uploaded, shared, or sold.

Data we share

None. TwirlNow does not transmit your LinkedIn data to any third party.

How to revoke

Revoke access at any time at linkedin.com/psettings/permitted-services.
